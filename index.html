Mutton Biryani

×

Ingredients of Mutton Biryani for the rice:

  • Star anise
  • 500 gms basmati rice, blanched
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 2 Black cardamom>
  • 2 tsp black cumin seeds
  • 6 Black peppercorn
  • 6 Green cardamom
  • 2 Cinnamon sticks
  • 6 Cloves
  • 1 tsp fennel
  • 1/4 Jaiphal
  • 1 Javitri
  • 3 tsp Salt
    • For mutton marination:
  • 1 kg mutton (cut in 2 inches pieces, preferably front leg part and avoid shoulder cut)
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 3 tbsp raw papaya paste
  • 4 tbsp hung curd
  • 1 Lemon (juiced)
  • Coriander powder
  • Cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp salt
    • Other ingredients:
  • 4 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup milk (warm)
  • Ghee
  • Saffron strands
  • Oil
  • Rose water
  • Kewra essence
  • 4 green chillies


    • How to Make Mutton Biryani:
    Marinate the mutton:
    1. 1.To the mutton add the beaten curd, ginger-garlic paste, raw papaya paste, chilli powder, salt, lemon juice, garam masala.
    2. the mutton to marinate for 3 hours.
      3. Prepare fried onions or Barista:
    3. 1. 2 onions very thinly. Separate the slices.
    4. 2.In a pan or kadai add oil and fry the onion slices till nicely brown.
    5. 3.Take care not to burn them. Fry in small batches. Do not put all the slices at a time, that will lead to lumpy messy onions.
    6. 4.Make sure all the onion slices frying are dipped in oil, if needed add more oil. Keep stirring continuously but gently for an even brown color.
    7. 5.Take the fried onions out with a slotted spoon or ladle.
    8. 6.Keep them on a paper-towel lined plate. This crispy brown fried onion slices are called Barista.
      9. Cook the mutton:
    9. 1.Heat ghee in a thick-bottomed pan.
    10. 2.Add remaining sliced onions and green chillies. Cook, stirring continuously, till onions are light golden brown.
    11. 3.Add ginger paste and garlic paste and mix well.
    12. 4.Add marinated mutton and cook on high heat for seven to eight minutes.
    13. 5.Add coriander powder, cumin powder and red chilli powder. Mix thoroughly.
    14. 6.Stir in three cups of water, bring it to a boil, reduce heat and cook covered till mutton is almost cooked.
    15. 7.Add tomatoes, salt, garam masala powder and fresh coriander leaves.
    16. 8.Cook for 15 minutes on medium heat, stirring occasionally. The ghee would be separated from the spices and there should not be any watery gravy to the meat.
      17. Prepare the rice:
    17. 1.Use only good quality long grain Basmati rice.
    18. 2. Soak the rice for 20 minutes in water.
    19. 3. Wash well till the water runs clear. Drain all the water.
    20. 4.In a small piece of cloth take cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, javitri, jaiphal, black peppercorn, shahi jeera, star anise and tie a knot to make a bag (potli).
    21. 5.Bring 750 ml water to boil, add rice, bay leaf, salt and potli, cover and cook till rice is done 1/3rd.
    22. 6.Drain the water & remove the whole masala potli.
      23. Prepare the saffron-milk:
    23. 1.Take 1/4th cup warm milk in a cup and dissolve saffron strands in the milk.
    24. 2.Cover and wait for 20 minutes. Add rose water and kewra essence in the milk. Mix well and cover. Keep aside.
      25. Layer the biryani:
    25. 1.take a large heavy bottom pan with tight fitting lid.
    26. 2.Add 2tbsp ghee to the pan. Melt the ghee on low heat. Turn and rotate the pan carefully so the ghee can coat the bottom and sides of the pan. Switch off the heat.
    27. 3.Add a layer of cooked rice, then cooked meat pieces, sprinkle saffron water, add fried onion slices and ghee.
    28. 4.Again add a layer of rice, then meat...go on like this till you are done. Top and bottom layer will be of rice.
    29. 5.Cover with chopped pudina and coriander, fried onion and slit green chillies and juice of half a lemon.
    30. 6.Put the lid on. Seal the pan with flour dough or aluminium foil, then put the lid. Keep the heat to lowest. And cook the Biryani in this 'Dum' process for 40 minutes.
    31. 7.Make sure your pan is heavy-bottomed or the rice will burn. Or you can place a flat tawa then keep the pan on that tawa.
    32. 8.After 40 minutes switch off the heat and let the biryani stand for another 10 minutes. Transfer to a serving bowl.
      33. serving:
    33. 1.Serve with raita and salad.(optional)
      34. You'r done!!

    Chicken Curry

    ×

    ingrediants for spicy chicken curry

  • 1 kilograms chicken
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala powder
  • 1 tablespoon kasoori methi powder
  • 2 1/2 cup water
  • 2 onion
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 cup cashews
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 3 red chilli
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • For Marination
  • salt as required
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
    • For Garnishing
  • 1/2 handful coriander leaves


      • Step 1 Wash the chicken & saute the whole spices
    1. 1.wash the chicken under running water and keep it aside.
    2. 2.take a non-stick pan and pour little oil in it.
    3. 3.Then add whole spices - cumin seeds, cinnamon seeds and bay leaf.
    4. 4.Let them cook until the seeds start to crackle.
      5. Step 2 Marinate the chicken & prepare onion, cashews & red chillies paste
    5. 1. marinate the chicken with salt, turmeric powder and lemon juice and keep it aside for 20-30 minutes.
    6. 2. using a grinder, grind onion and 1 cup of water to make a puree of thick consistency.
    7. 3. Put aside till required.
    8. 4. Wash the grinder and put cashews in it and add 1 cup of water.
    9. 5. Make a paste of cashews of smooth consistency.
    10. 6.wash the grinder and grind red chillies with 1/2 cup of water to form a paste of thick consistency.
      11. Step 3 Cook the onion & tomato puree with the lid on
    11. 1. in the pan with the whole spices, add onion puree with ginger and garlic paste and cook it for 2 minutes.
    12. 2.Add tomato puree to the pan and cook for another 2 minutes.
    13. 3.Meanwhile, add salt to taste and red chilli paste.
    14. 4.Cover the pan with a lid.
      15. Step 4 Add the marinated chicken & cashews & red chilli paste
    15. 1.add the marinated chicken pieces in the gravy and let it cook for more 15-20 minutes with the lid on the pan
    16. 2.after 15-20 minutes, check if the chicken pieces are cooked properly.
    17. 3.add cashews and red chilli paste into the mixture and cook for another 2 minutes.
      18. Step 5 Add the spices & cook the chicken
    18. 1.add garam masala, coriander powder, cashew nut paste and Kasuri methi and mix them all well.
    19. 2.Let the chicken curry cook for another 5 minutes and then turn off the flame.
    20. 3.Garnish the curry with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with steamed rice or paratha.


      21. you'r done!!

    Fried Chicken

    ×

    ingredients for fried chicken

  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into 10 pieces
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 quart buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • vegetable oil for deep frying


    • step 1:
    1. 1.Sprinkle chicken pieces with salt and pepper; place them in a deep-sided baking dish.
      2. step 2:
    2. 1.Whisk buttermilk, hot pepper sauce, and 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper together in a large bowl.
    3. 2.pour over chicken, tossing to coat all sides.
    4. 3.Cover the dish with plastic wrap; marinate in the refrigerator for about 2 hours.
      5. step 3:
    5. 1.Remove chicken from buttermilk mixture; shake off excess.
    6. 2.Discard remaining buttermilk mixture.
      7. step 4:
    7. 1.Place flour, 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a large resealable plastic bag.
    8. 2.shake well to combine.
    9. Place 2 or 3 pieces of chicken at a time into the plastic bag and shake well to coat chicken pieces with seasoned flour.
      10. step 5:
    10. 1.Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
      11. step 6:
    11. 1.Gently place chicken pieces into hot oil and fry until chicken is cooked through and golden brown.
    12. 2.About 8 to10 minutes for breasts and wings, and 13 to 15 minutes for thighs and drumsticks.
    13. 3.An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
    14. 4.Drain fried chicken on paper towels.
      15. You'r done!!

    Pasta

    ×

    Boiling the pasta step 1:

    1. 1.Fill a large pot about 2/3 full of water.
    2. 2.Since the pasta needs lots of room to move around as it cooks, use a big pot.
    3. 3.For example, if you'll be cooking a 1 pound (450 g) box of pasta, get out a pot that's at least 4 US quarts (3.8 L) in size. Then, pour in enough water to come 2/3 up the side of the pot.
    4. 4.If you use pot that's too small, your pasta is more likely to clump together as it cooks.
      5. step 2:
    5. 1.Cover the pot and bring the water to a boil.
    6. 2.Set the pot of water on the stove and put a lid on it.
    7. 3.Turn the burner to high and let the water come to a boil.
    8. 4.You'll know the water is boiling when you see steam escaping from under the lid.
    9. 5.Covering the pot with a lid will make the water boil faster.
      10. step 3:
    10. 1.Add salt and 1 pound (450 g) of pasta to the boiling water.
    11. 2.Once the water boils vigorously, remove the lid and add 1 tablespoon (17 g) of salt and 1 pound (450 g) of pasta.
    12. If you're cooking long noodles, such as spaghetti, that don't fit in the pot, wait about 30 seconds and then push them into the water using a spoon or pasta fork.
    13. The salt will season the noodles as they cook, which will give you flavorful pasta.
    14. If you're unsure how many servings of pasta to cook, check the side of the box for recommended serving sizes.
      15. step 4:
    15. 1.Set a timer for 3 to 8 minutes.
    16. 2.Stir the noodles with a pasta fork to break the noodles up and leave the lid off the pot.
    17. 3.Then, check the pasta package for a recommended cooking time and set a timer for the minimum time that's suggested.
    18. 4.For example, if the box says to cook the noodles for 7 to 9 minutes, set a timer for 7 minutes.
    19. 5.Thin pasta, such as angel hair, will cook faster than thick or long noodles, such as fettuccine or penne, which take closer to 8 or 9 minutes.
      20. step 5:
    20. 1.Stir the noodles occasionally as they boil.
    21. 2.The water should continue to bubble as the pasta cooks.
    22. 3.Stir it every few minutes to prevent the noodles from sticking together.
    23. 4.If the water looks like it might boil over the sides of the pot, turn the burner down to medium-high.
    24. 5.Bite into a noodle to see if it's cooked enough for you.
      25. step 6:
    25. 1.Scoop out about 1 cup (240 ml) of pasta water and set it aside.
    26. 2.Set a colander in the sink and put on oven mitts.
    27. 3.Pour the pasta into the colander and shake it.
    28. 4.Avoid adding oil or running cold water over the pasta if you plan on using sauce.
    29. 5.Put the pasta back into the pot and toss it with your choice of sauce.
      30. You'r done!!

    Masala Dosa

    ×

    ingredients for masala dosa

  • 2 cup parboiled rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup refined oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
    • For Filling:
  • 1/2 kilograms boiled potato
  • 2 medium chopped green chilli
  • 1 tablespoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • salt as required
  • 1/2 cup sliced onion
  • 2 tablespoon refined oil
  • 10 leaves curry leaves
  • 1/4 cup water


    • Step 1 Prepare the batter and ferment overnight
    1. 1.To prepare the batter for Masala Dosa, wash and soak the rice (with fenugreek added into it) and urad dal in separate containers for approximately 6-8 hours.
    2. 2.Once the rice and urad dal are soaked well, grind them separately in a mixer using the water in which they were soaked, till the mixture reaches a smooth consistency.
    3. 3.Mix the batter of both the ingredients in a bigger container and add salt to it.
    4. 4.Combine well and allow it to ferment overnight.
      5. Step 2 Prepare the potato filling for Masala Dosa
    5. 1.To prepare the filling of the dosa, heat 2 tbsp oil in a thick-bottomed pan and let the mustard seeds splutter.
    6. 2.add sliced onions, curry leaves, green chillies and saute them till the onions turn pink.
    7. 3.add a pinch of salt, turmeric powder and mix them well.
    8. 4.take the cubed potatoes and add them to the sauteed onions and mix them together.
    9. 5.Pour water gradually into the mixture and allow the potatoes to simmer for around 4 minutes.
    10. 6.When the mixture is in semi-thick state, turn off the gas and let it rest for a few seconds.
      11. Step 3 Pan fry your dosa on a dosa tawa
    11. 1.take a dosa tawa and heat it on low-medium flame.
    12. 2.Smear 1 tsp oil on it to prepare the dosa.
    13. 3.When the tawa is hot, pour and spread the batter evenly in a circular motion.
      14. Step 4 Add filling and fold the dosa
    14. 1.When the colour of dosa's edges turns into brown, reduce the flame and sprinkle few drops of oil on the dosa sides and put 2 tablespoons of filling.
    15. 2.Fold the dosa. Repeat the process until all the batter and filling is used up.
    16. 3.Serve hot Masala Dosa with coconut chutney and sambhar.(optional)

    Soup

    ×

    ingredients for hot & sour soup

  • 8 cups chicken broth or vegetable broth
  • 8 ounces shiitake mushrooms (or baby bella mushrooms), thinly-sliced with stems discarded
  • 1 (8-ounce) can bamboo shoots, drained (optional)
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar, or more to taste
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon chili garlic sauce
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2 large eggs, whisked
  • 8 ounces firm tofu*, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • Kosher salt and white pepper (or black pepper)
    • step 1:
    1. 1.Set aside ¼ cup of the chicken or vegetable broth for later use.
    2. 2.Add the remaining 7 ¾ cups chicken or vegetable broth, mushrooms, bamboo shoots (if using), rice wine vinegar, soy sauce, ginger and chili garlic sauce to a large stock pot, and stir to combine.
    3. 3.heat over medium-high heat until the soup reaches a simmer.
      4. step 2:
    4. 1.While the soup is heating, whisk together the ¼ cup of broth (that you had set aside) and cornstarch in a small bowl until completely smooth.
      5. Once the soup has reached a simmer, stir in the cornstarch mixture and stir for 1 minute or so until the soup has thickened.
    5. Continue stirring the soup in a circular motion, then drizzle in the eggs in a thin stream (while still stirring the soup) to create egg ribbons.
    6. Stir in the tofu, half of the green onions, and sesame oil. Then season the soup with salt and a pinch* of white pepper (or black pepper) to taste. If you’d like a more “sour” soup, feel free to add in another tablespoon or two of rice wine vinegar as well. Or if you’d like a spicier soup, add in more chili garlic sauce.
    7. Serve immediately, garnished with the extra green onions.
      8. You'r done!!
